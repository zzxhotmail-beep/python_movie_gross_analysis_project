# 🎬 Movie Box Office Analysis | Data Analyst Portfolio Project (Python)

## 📌 Business Problem

What drives a movie’s box office success?

Studios invest millions in production and marketing, but predicting financial performance remains challenging. This project analyzes key factors influencing movie gross revenue, helping stakeholders make more informed investment decisions.

## 🎯 Objective

- Identify the strongest predictors of box office revenue
- Quantify relationships between movie features and gross earnings
- Translate data insights into actionable business recommendations

## 📊 Dataset

- Source: Kaggle Movie industry dataset （[Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/danielgrijalvas/movies)）
- Includes:
  - Budget
  - Gross revenue
  - Votes (audience engagement)
  - Additional movie attributes (genre, year, director, company, etc.)

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Demonstrated

- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy)
- **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing**
- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
- **Data Visualization** (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- **Statistical Analysis** (Pearson Correlation)
- **Business Insight Generation**

## 🔍 Key Analysis

1. **Correlation Analysis**:
   - Computed Pearson correlation coefficients between numerical variables
   - Visualized relationships using heatmaps and scatter plots

2. **Feature Impact on Revenue**:
   - Compared budget, votes, and other variables against gross earnings
   - Identified strongest drivers of financial performance

## 📈 Key Insights

- **Budget is the strongest predictor** of box office revenue (**correlation: 0.74**)
- **Audience engagement (votes)** also shows strong influence (**correlation: 0.61**)
- High-budget films tend to generate higher revenue, supported by strong audience engagement 

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- 🎯 **Increase strategic investment in high-potential projects**  
→ Higher budgets correlate with higher returns
- 📣 **Focus on audience engagement & marketing**  
→ Votes indicate popularity and directly impact revenue
- 📊 **Use data-driven decision-making in greenlighting films**  
→ Combine financial and engagement metrics for better predictions
